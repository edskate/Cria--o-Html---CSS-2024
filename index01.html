    Auxiliary verbos:

    O inglês utiliza verbos auxiliares na construção de algumas frases. São eles:
    to be(ser/estar) To have(ter) to do {fazer}
    I am I have I do
    you are you have you do
    He/she/it is He/ she/ it has He/she/it does
    We are We have We do
    They are They have They do

    Para formar frases \negativas, Basta acrescentar o 'Not'

    To be (ser/estar) To have (ter) To do (fazer)
    I am not I have not I do not
    You are not You have not You do not
    He/she/it is not He/she/it has not He/she/it does not
    we are not we have not we do not
    They are not They have not They do not
